# 🧠 Why Programmers Prefer Mechanical Keyboards

## 🔥 1. Better Typing Accuracy

Mechanical switches provide **tactile feedback**, helping you know exactly when a key is pressed.

👉 This reduces typing errors and improves coding speed.

---

## ⚡ 2. Long-Term Durability

Most mechanical keyboards are rated for:

- ✅ 50–100 million keystrokes
- ✅ Years of heavy usage

👉 Perfect for developers who type all day.

---

## 🎨 3. Customization Options

Mechanical keyboards allow:

- Switch replacement
- Custom keycaps
- RGB lighting

👉 You can build your **perfect typing experience**

---

## 🪑 4. Reduced Typing Fatigue

Better ergonomics = less strain.

- Smooth key travel
- Comfortable typing angle

👉 Ideal for long coding sessions.

# 🧠 How to Choose the Best Mechanical Keyboard

Choosing the right keyboard depends on your needs.

## 🎯 1. Switch Type

- 🔵 Blue → Clicky (loud)
- 🟤 Brown → Balanced
- 🔴 Red → Smooth & silent

👉 **Best for programmers:** Brown or Red switches

---

## 📏 2. Keyboard Size

- Full size → All keys
- TKL (Tenkeyless) → No numpad
- 60% → Compact

👉 Best choice: **TKL or 75%**

---

## 🔌 3. Wired vs Wireless

- Wired → No latency
- Wireless → Clean setup

👉 Choose based on your setup style.

---

## 💡 4. Build Quality

Look for:

- Aluminum frame
- Strong keycaps
- Stable switches
