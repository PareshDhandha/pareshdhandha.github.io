# 🧠 Buying Guide (Important Before You Buy)

Choosing the right earbuds depends on your needs.

---

## 🎧 1. Active Noise Cancellation (ANC)

- Best for travel & noisy environments
- Blocks background noise

👉 Recommended: At least **25–30dB ANC**

---

## 🔋 2. Battery Life

Look for:

- Minimum: 30 hours
- Ideal: 40–50 hours

👉 Fast charging is a big bonus ⚡

---

## 🎮 3. Gaming Mode / Low Latency

Important for:

- Gamers
- Video watching

👉 Reduces audio delay

---

## 🎤 4. Call Quality

Look for:

- ENC or Quad Mic setup
- Clear voice in noisy areas

---

## 📶 5. Bluetooth Version

Minimum:

- Bluetooth 5.1  
  Best:

- Bluetooth 5.3 or above
